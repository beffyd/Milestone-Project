Rose's Flower Shop started in 1988 and was founded by Rose Adkinson who was from Tottenham in North London.

She got into flower arranging at a young age as her father was a pastor at the church, as she grew up she would help the ladies with the flower displays.
She strived to create beautiful flower displays for events such as weddings, birthdays, fairs and more!

After Rose's passing in 2001, her daughter Stephanie Adkinson has been running the family business, catering to weddings, events, birthdays and all sorts of flower related needs.
